Information Collection and Use

The Basic Info app displays basic device information such as CPU usage percentage. All processing happens locally on your device.

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

No information is shared with external servers or services.

Any device information accessed by the app remains on your device and is used only to display CPU usage.

Log Data

In case of an app error, the operating system may generate technical crash logs. This may include:

Device name

Operating system version

App configuration at the time of the error

Time and date of the error

This information is used solely for diagnosing and improving app performance. The app itself does not collect these logs.

Cookies

The Basic Info app does not use cookies. Some system-level components may use cookies, but the app does not access or store them.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use advertisements.

The app does not use third-party analytics.

No personal information is shared with any external services.

If the device platform automatically sends crash reports, they may contain non-personal technical data only.

Security
